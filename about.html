Our Story

Excellence since 2016.

Founded in Nairobi in 2016, Machuka Okello Advocates began as a boutique corporate practice before expanding into a full-service law firm serving clients across eight practice areas. Our growth has been built entirely on client referrals and the results we deliver — a testament to our commitment to doing things right, every time.

Today, our team of experienced advocates operates from our Nairobi headquarters to serve clients across Kenya, the East African Community, and international markets. We maintain an unwavering dedication to client service, ethical practice, and innovative legal thinking.
